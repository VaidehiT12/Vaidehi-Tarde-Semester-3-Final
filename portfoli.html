For this brand project, I worked with Paper Boat, focusing on creating a small advertising campaign that highlighted the brand's playful and nostalgic identity. Through clever and engaging visuals and creative messaging, I aimed to evoke a sense of warmth and comfort, reflective of the brand's values and positioning. The project ultimately achieved its goal, resonating with the target audience and effectively communicating the brand's unique story and personality.
